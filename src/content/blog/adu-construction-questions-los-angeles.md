ADU construction in Los Angeles can create flexible living space, rental potential, or room for family, but the project needs a realistic site review before construction begins.

Garage conversions, detached ADUs, and compact backyard homes each carry different questions around structure, utilities, access, setbacks, fire separation, and inspections.

econstruct helps homeowners think through the construction realities so the ADU path is practical, buildable, and coordinated.

## Early ADU questions

- Is the project a garage conversion, detached ADU, or addition?
- Where will utilities connect?
- How will crews access the work area?
- Are there slope, parking, or fire-access constraints?
- What drawings or approvals already exist?

## Why it matters

The best ADU projects start with feasibility, then move into design, permitting, trade coordination, and construction. Skipping those early questions can create cost and schedule problems later.
